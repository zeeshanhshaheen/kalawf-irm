Who is Fahad

A lawyer by profession, a social worker by choice and a Philanthropist at heart.

For the past decade I have been working with the people and for the people to find solutions to their problems. By protecting the vulnerable, promoting social change and development and empowering people and communities. All that we have achieved would not have been possible without the support of everyone that has joined hands with us over the years. The common men and women, aren’t so common after all. I wholeheartedly thank you all for the love and unwavering support and trust in me. Together we can bring a change.
